XHILO

UI/UX - event identity - motion - team work
co-ideator, UI/UX designer, motion artist

A proposal for a contemporary music festival that focuses around experiencing music in an immersive environment and building a supportive community.

Goal

The goal of XHILO’s branding was to create a distinguishable identity people would easily recognise. While discussing our own needs when attending festivals, my teammates and I noticed that festivals often don’t offer many opportunities to easily meet new people, as the majority of attendees already arrives in their own groups. We wanted to create a brand for a festival that would promote that, and allow people to feel that they all are a part of a bigger XHILO community.


Persona, community

XHILO’s target audience are young adults who are seeking an event connecting their passion for music and their need of finding meaningful connections with others through common experiences.

Motion Graphics

Expanding our design system into the realm of animation was an important part to highlighting the festival’s immersive aspect. Including motion helped me emphasise the time aspect of immersion.


social media teaser for the festival

3D

As a teaser for the social media account I developed a 3D animation incorporating our main symbol morphing in and out followed by the festival’s name. The animation together with the sound is supposed to get the viewer excited for the event and symbolically set their expectations for the progression of the event shown through the sounds.

Using innovative visuals beyond the 2D were identified as an important aspect of drawing a young demographic in and making them interested in the festival.

2D

The posters were also created to be motion graphics that could advertise XHILO on social media and other digital platforms. By creating simple, dynamic animations, the posters would promote the event and engage the viewers with the content.

Event app

The app is a medium to convey the spatial aspect of immersion through. Serving as the attendees’ aid in all stages of XHILO: preparation before, participation during, and reminiscing after it helps fascilitate connection to the festival itself. It functions both as a personal hub for the participant’s schedule and interesting activities, and also as a platform to connect people with one another.


Before attending

The personal preferences selected in the beginning of the app experience serve as data for the app’s AI to suggest people and experiences that might be of interest to the user. The machine learning would personalise everything from concert suggestions, through people to connect with to social activites the user could participate in.

During the event

This part of the app experience focuses on concert and activity participation and management. Some of the main features of this stage are the calendar with favourited events, the map, and the programme page suggesting new events.

Staying connected

Once XHILO festival is over, the attendees are able to browse highlights from their friends, access statistics on attendance during different events, and be able to sign up for a waiting list to purchase tickets for the next edition.
